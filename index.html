About This App

The Clarens Heritage Trail brings the stories of historic buildings, rock art, and landscapes to life — right in your hands as you walk the village streets.

This is an early prototype for internal review. Full v1.0 will include GPS navigation, audio guides, and a complete site database.

About the CHA

Established in 2024, the Clarens Heritage Association is a voluntary association dedicated to fostering, conserving, and promoting all aspects of heritage in Clarens and surrounds.

Blue Plaques

The HASA Blue Plaque is a distinctive circular marker celebrating places of cultural, historical, or architectural significance. Look for them on buildings as you walk the trail.
